Welcome to Blumerq

With a distinguished legacy of over 15 years, BLUMERQ Hospitality is a leading hotel investment, ownership, and management firm. Our diverse portfolio includes a broad spectrum of properties, such as independent and branded upscale hotels, resorts, medical centers, luxury homes across Southern California, agricultural land, wineries, and retirement community properties.

See our projects

At BLUMERQ Hospitality, we prioritize the strength of partnerships, teamwork, and collaboration. Our company culture, shaped over many years, is built on values of excellence, inclusivity, and support. We are committed to fostering an environment that promotes both personal and professional growth, while ensuring a balanced work-life environment for every team member.

Our commitment extends beyond exceptional guest service, aiming to make a meaningful and positive impact on our employees, clients, colleagues, and the communities in which we operate. The success of BLUMERQ Hospitality is driven by our exceptional people, who consistently deliver outstanding experiences and results.
