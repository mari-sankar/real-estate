Welcome to Blumerq

More than one decade of experience, BLUMERQ INVESTMENT GROUP set out to be the best investment advisor, finder and operator in commercial sector all over USA/Canada. Through valued partnerships with internationally recognized hotel brands, BLUMERQ grew a portfolio focusing on running successful select and full service hotels. BLUMERQ came with one goal, to create the best customer satisfaction and guaranteed returns for their investments. Ten years later, BLUMERQ owns and operates 10+ unique commercial assets such as Full Service Hotels, Medical Center Buildings, Office Building in prime locations, Beach/Vacation Homes for Corporate Rentals and Film Studios with more coming nationwide.
